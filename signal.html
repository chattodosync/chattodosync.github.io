The Signal

Why we built Chattodo

This is our signal. A message to everyone who values clarity, focus, and meaningful work. This is why Chattodo exists.

Noise Solved

Chattodo is a messaging app with three features.

Chat. Todo. Team. That's it.
  • Chat: Messaging. Simple, real-time, human to human.
  • Todo: Double-tap messages to turn them into tasks.
  • Team: Your main 5 & you. 5+1=6 users per circle.

Every additional feature and every additional entity in apps is a decision point for the user. More decisions to make means more mechanical interruptions in the flow of your pure thought.

The Question

What if 'just chatting' exactly meant 'just working'?

Chattodo is built for:

  • Small teams: that can fit in 1 car.
  • 1 action: 0 configuration.
  • No learning curve: You already know chatting.

Users must be mindful of who they onboard. Not everyone should get a seat in their car.

Moving Mountains

Small teams that are efficient by use of technology can move mountains together. In some cases, perhaps just one person 'teams' will be building billions of dollars worth of companies. Such is this age in tech.

Chattodo is for builders who want a quiet room with their main five — away from privacy compromises, integrations, bots, and feature bloat.

Transparency & Accountability

We have designed the Chattodo mobile app to help our users accomplish increased transparency and accountability in their team.

Similarly, our users must expect absolute transparency and accountability from us. We don't feed the user's data to machine learning. The user's data is their property alone.

We have built Chattodo because we needed a minimalistic temple of work for our remote team to chat in. Now we are sharing it.

In the world of AI, we are offering our users a productive experience without the noise of people and AI. We are anti-bloat. We are for humans of clarity.

The Sum

Only human to human chats, with only 6 people, through only 3 core features. Full stop.

Noise killed indeed.

Trust

Trust is not an afterthought. It is key. We are actively building Chattodo towards full privacy-by-design and ethical product principles — because that's the standard our users deserve.

If this resonates with you, Chattodo is yours to try.
