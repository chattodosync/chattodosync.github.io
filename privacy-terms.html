Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 5, 2026

Chattodo ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ("App"). Please read this policy carefully.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you sign up using Google Sign-In, we collect your name, email address, and profile picture as provided by Google. You may also provide a display name, handle, bio, avatar, and personal links within the App.

Messages & Content

We store the messages you send and receive, including text messages, images, videos, GIFs, audio recordings, files, documents, contacts, and location data that you choose to share. Messages may be marked as tasks (todos) with assignment and status information.

Usage Data

We collect information about how you interact with the App, including features used, tasks created, messages sent, and general usage patterns. This helps us improve the App experience.

Device Information

We may collect device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers for the purpose of delivering push notifications and ensuring App compatibility.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the messaging and task management service
  • To deliver messages, notifications, and task updates in real-time
  • To enable features such as search, mentions, reactions, and task assignment
  • To manage your account, profile, connections, and preferences
  • To improve, personalize, and develop new features for the App
  • To communicate with you about updates, changes, or issues

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored using Google Firebase Cloud Firestore. Messages are stored directly in Cloud Firestore and are not end-to-end encrypted. Firebase provides infrastructure-level security including encryption in transit (TLS) and encryption at rest for stored data. Access to your data is controlled through Firebase security rules and authentication.

While we rely on Firebase's security infrastructure, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security and encourage users to be mindful of the information they share.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Your information may be shared in the following limited circumstances:

  • With other users: Your profile information (name, handle, avatar, bio, online status) is visible to users you connect with, subject to your privacy preferences.
  • Service providers: We use Google Firebase for infrastructure. Their privacy policies apply to data processed through their services.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or in response to valid legal requests.

5. Your Privacy Controls

Chattodo provides you with privacy controls including:

  • Choosing whether your email is publicly visible
  • Controlling your online status visibility
  • Controlling your last seen visibility
  • Blocking users
  • Muting conversations
  • Deleting messages (for yourself or for everyone)

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. If you delete your account, we will remove your personal data, though some information may be retained in backups for a limited period or as required by law.

7. Children's Privacy

Chattodo is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected such information, we will take steps to delete it promptly.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please reach out to us through the App.
